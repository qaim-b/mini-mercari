# Mini Mercari One-Go Execution Plan

## Mission
Build a smooth "switch from Mercari to Assistant App and back" experience that helps users find what they need faster, with higher confidence and less effort.

## Product Positioning
- Not a full marketplace replacement.
- This is a "Mercari Listing and Discovery Assistant".
- It improves user outcomes before, during, and after listing/search actions.

## One Sentence Value Proposition
"In under 60 seconds, users get better listing quality, better price confidence, and faster item discovery."

## North Star
Increase successful listing outcomes and discovery efficiency for Mercari users.

## 6-Week Outcomes (Must Hit)
1. 30% reduction in time to publish a listing (pilot users).
2. 20% increase in listing completeness score.
3. 15% increase in search-to-action conversion in pilot flow.
4. >70% "useful" rating on suggestions.

## Scope Lock
### In Scope
- Listing assistant: title/category/description/price help.
- Discovery assistant: better filter + intent-based suggestions.
- Frictionless account session and switch flow.
- Analytics + feedback loop.

### Out of Scope
- Full checkout and payment replacement.
- Full order lifecycle parity with Mercari.
- General social/community features.

## Operating Model
- Weekly sprint cadence.
- Pilot-first shipping.
- Every release tied to measurable KPI changes.
